Veggie Pakora

Description

Vegetable pakoras, also known as bhaji, are similar to poppers in that they are deep-fried and have a similar texture. They're made with potatoes, onions, cauliflower, and even eggplant, among other veggies. With a cup of tea or coffee, these sweet and spicy delicacies are generally served.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpea flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 quart oil for deep frying
  • 2 onions, sliced into rings

Steps

  1. Sift the chickpea flour into a medium bowl. Mix in the coriander, salt, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala and garlic.

  2. Make a well in the center of the flower. Gradually pour the water into the well and mix to form a thick, smooth batter./li>
  3. Over medium high heat in a large, heavy saucepan, heat the oil to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  4. Coat the cauliflower and onions in the batter and fry them in small batches until golden brown, about 4 to 5 minutes. Drain on paper towels before serving.
